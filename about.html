SYLVANGEL MARITIME RESOURCES

SYLVANGEL MARITIME RESOURCES LTD RC No. 867356 is a Limited Liability company duly incorporated under the corporate and allied Matters Act of 1990, with the sole aim of contributing to the nation’s economic development through the provision of qualitative services in the Maritime Industry, sector. We are NIMASA accredited crewing/manning Agent, with a pool of carefully selected seafarers that have been trained to render quality services and adhere strictly to international safety standards on board vessels. Our good rapport with the Nigeria Maritime Administration And Safety (NIMASA)and the ability to effectively manage community related issues that are peculiar with the Niger Delta Region are some of the advantages clients enjoy in doing business with us.
WINNER BEST MANNING AND CREWING AGENT 2017

SYLVANGEL MARITIME RESOURCES LTD is a registered marine servicing company driven by goal oriented service delivery principles that guarantee maximum value and clients’ satisfaction. Our affordable services and technical know-how epitomize reliability and dependability as jobs are delivered within specified time frame which must always be adhered to. More so, the need for improving standard and values in maritime industry in this present generation cannot be over-emphasized since the world today now calls for prompt response to service delivery. Hence, in light of this, SYLVANGEL MARITIME RESOURCES LTD is established solely among others, to recover that lost value and ethics in maritime operations.
